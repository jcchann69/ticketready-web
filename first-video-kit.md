# TicketReady First Video Kit

## Video Goal

Make one short video that proves the product idea in under 30 seconds:

> "Can you solve a real help desk ticket before the score reveals if you are job-ready?"

Use the first ticket in the prototype: `Payroll app stuck in MFA loop`.

## Final Video Format

- Length: 20 to 30 seconds
- Size: vertical 9:16
- Platforms: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- Style: screen recording plus captions and voiceover
- CTA: "Try today's ticket"

## Exact Script

### 0-2 seconds

On-screen text:

> Would you pass this help desk ticket?

Voiceover:

> Would you pass this real help desk ticket?

### 2-7 seconds

Show the ticket text.

On-screen text:

> Payroll deadline. New phone. MFA lockout.

Voiceover:

> The user got a new phone, payroll closes at 4 PM, and MFA is stuck on the old authenticator.

### 7-12 seconds

Show category and priority fields.

On-screen text:

> What is the first move?

Voiceover:

> Do you reset the password, bypass MFA, or verify identity first?

### 12-20 seconds

Click the correct workflow:

1. Category: Access
2. Priority: P2
3. Verify identity
4. Reset MFA registration
5. Confirm resolution
6. Document resolution

On-screen text:

> Correct path: verify identity -> reset MFA -> confirm -> document

Voiceover:

> The job-ready path is verify identity, reset MFA, confirm payroll access, and document everything.

### 20-27 seconds

Show the score result.

On-screen text:

> 98/100 job-ready resolution

Voiceover:

> That scores 98 out of 100. This is the kind of practice that builds real help desk confidence.

### 27-30 seconds

Show the Pro Outcome or Job Evidence section.

On-screen text:

> Try today's ticket: TicketReady

Voiceover:

> Try today's ticket and see if you can beat the score.

## Recording Steps

1. Open the prototype at `http://127.0.0.1:8787/`.
2. Start on the first ticket: `Payroll app stuck in MFA loop`.
3. Record your screen in vertical framing if possible.
4. Slowly scroll or position the ticket so the customer note is readable.
5. Select `Access`.
6. Select `P2`.
7. Click:
   - `Verify identity`
   - `Reset MFA registration`
   - `Confirm resolution`
   - `Document resolution`
8. Click `Submit Resolution`.
9. Pause on the score screen for 2 seconds.
10. Pause on the Job Evidence Lab for 2 seconds.

## Editing Steps In CapCut

1. Create a new vertical project.
2. Import the screen recording.
3. Cut dead time between clicks.
4. Add the on-screen text from the script.
5. Use auto captions for the voiceover.
6. Add a light background track at low volume.
7. Add a quick zoom on:
   - the customer note
   - the correct action choices
   - the score
8. Export as 1080x1920 MP4.

## AI Tools You Can Use

- ChatGPT or Claude: generate more hooks and variations.
- CapCut: edit, auto captions, text-to-speech, templates.
- Canva: thumbnail, title frame, simple graphics.
- ElevenLabs: polished voiceover if you do not want to record your own voice.
- Descript: clean voice audio and remove filler words.
- OpusClip: repurpose a longer walkthrough into short clips.

## Success Metric For This First Video

Do not judge only by views. The real signal is:

- Did people comment their answer?
- Did anyone ask for the next ticket?
- Did anyone click the site?
- Did anyone join the waitlist?

If comments are weak, make the next video more interactive by asking viewers to choose `A`, `B`, or `C`.
